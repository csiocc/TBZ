# 7 Zip Factsheet

  ## General Infos
    -Developer: Igor Pavlov
    -First release: 1999
    -Website: 7-Zip.org
  ## Keyfeatures
    -Good Compression
    -Encryption supports strong AES256
    -Provides better compression for ZIP and GZIP formats compared to PKZip and WinZip.
    -Erzeugt Passwörter mit random salt + SHA 256 Hash verschlüsselung
### -Supported Formats
      -Packing/unpacking: 7z, XZ, BZIP2, GZIP, TAR, ZIP, and WIM.
      -Unpack only: APFS, AR, ARJ, CAB, CHM, CPIO, CramFS, DMG, EXT, FAT, GPT, HFS, IHEX, ISO, LZH, LZMA,  
      MBR, MSI, NSIS, NTFS, QCOW2, RAR, RPM, SquashFS, UDF, UEFI, VDI, VHD, VHDX, VMDK, XAR, and Z.

### -User interface:
      -Benutzeroberfläche für einfaches Dateimanagement
      -Windowsshell Integration
      -CLI Version für Automation
